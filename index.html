Interactive Visualization

This project involves creating four informative charts using D3.js on different datasets about plastic pollution across the world and in specific countries over different time spans.

A Line chart which shows the Plastic Waste produced by the whole world from 1950 to 2019 in tons.

View Chart

A Choropleth Map which shows share of global plastic waste emitted to the ocean for the year 2019.

View Chart

A Stacked Bar Chart which shows the annual shares of waste management methods from 2000 to 2019.

View Chart

A bubble chart which shows Mismanaged Plastic Waste per capita (kg per year) by countries.
